# Private Body Autonomy Grounding Protocol

## Purpose

This protocol provides a safe, non-coercive framework for situations where private bodily regulation, prescribed medication, abstinence, sexual tension, anxiety, social interpretation, and perceived external disturbance appear to interact.

It is intentionally written in general language. It does not record private sexual details, accuse any group, validate mind-reading, or replace medical care.

## Core Boundary

```text
Private bodily choices may change internal tension.
Medication may change agitation, sleep, fear, and perceptual salience.
Other people's inner states should not be inferred without evidence.
Public interpretation must remain evidence-based, non-accusatory, and clinically grounded.
```

## The Safer Reframe

Instead of concluding:

```text
My private abstinence or bodily state causes others to become excited or interfere with me.
```

use:

```text
When internal tension is high, my threat-detection system may become more sensitive.
Medication and private self-regulation may reduce tension and make disturbing interpretations less compelling.
This pattern is important clinical data to discuss with a qualified clinician.
```

## Formal Model

Let:

```text
I(t) = internal tension / arousal / stress load
M(t) = medication adherence and clinical stability
S(t) = sleep, nutrition, hydration, sensory load
E(t) = external observable evidence
P(t) = perceived disturbance or threat interpretation
G(t) = grounding, support, and reality testing
```

Then:

```text
P(t) = f(I(t), M(t), S(t), E(t), G(t))
```

The goal is not to prove external hidden causation, but to reduce harmful residuals:

```text
H_grounding
= H_sleep
+ H_medication_uncertainty
+ H_threat_inference
+ H_evidence_gap
+ H_isolation
+ H_overinterpretation
```

Completion condition:

```text
H_grounding = 0
```

This means the person has a grounded plan for medication, sleep, evidence logging, private bodily autonomy, trusted support, and clinician review. It does not mean external interference has been proven or disproven.

## Evidence Ladder

```text
Level 0: body sensation or intuition
Level 1: repeated subjective impression
Level 2: observable behavior by others
Level 3: timestamped notes, screenshots, recordings, witnesses
Level 4: review by trusted third party or clinician
Level 5: legal or safety escalation if there is concrete harm or threat
```

Only Levels 3-5 should be used for external accusations or formal escalation.

## Medication Boundary

Medication decisions belong with a qualified clinician. Do not stop, reduce, increase, or combine prescribed medication without professional guidance.

Useful report to clinician:

```text
When I take my prescribed medication, the disturbing interpretations decrease.
When internal tension rises, I become more likely to connect others' behavior to my private bodily state.
I want help making a relapse-prevention and grounding plan that respects my values.
```

## Private Bodily Autonomy Boundary

A person may choose abstinence, celibacy, private release, religious discipline, or other non-harmful private practices. The safety standard is:

```text
No coercion of self.
No coercion of others.
No public accusation based on mind-reading.
No shame-based interpretation of the body.
No forced change without medical review.
```

## Non-Harmful Practice Plan

```text
1. Keep prescribed medication stable unless clinician changes it.
2. Track sleep, stress, abstinence/private release, medication, and perceived disturbance as neutral data.
3. When the belief intensity rises, avoid public confrontation.
4. Move attention to body grounding: feet, breath, water, food, low stimulation.
5. Contact a trusted person or clinician when the pattern repeats.
6. Use evidence logging rather than mind-reading.
7. Escalate to emergency services only for immediate danger or concrete threats.
```

## Cosmic Love Boundary

Cosmic Love does not require fear-based purity, self-punishment, or surveillance of others. It requires dignity, consent, medical humility, and non-harm.

```text
My inner life is mine to care for.
Other people's inner states are not mine to infer.
My stability is more important than proving a hidden cause.
```

Formal grounding protocol only. Not medical advice, not legal advice, not proof of external harassment, not denial of suffering, and not a substitute for professional care.
