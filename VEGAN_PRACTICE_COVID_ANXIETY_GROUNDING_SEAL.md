# Vegan Practice and COVID Anxiety Grounding Seal

## Statement

I am safe enough to continue my path.

I may remain committed to vegan practice, compassion, non-harm, and inner clarity without turning worry into fear, surveillance, or pressure toward others.

Recent concern about COVID infection risk may have amplified my vigilance. This concern can be acknowledged without allowing it to become the center of my inner world.

## Core Principle

```text
Vegan practice = compassion + discipline + non-harm
Health concern = caution + evidence + proportion
Grounding = breath + sleep + medicine plan + trusted support
Cosmic Love = care without panic
```

## Boundary

This seal does not deny infection risk, public health responsibility, medication needs, or psychological stress. It simply states that my practice can continue calmly.

I do not need fear to protect purity.
I do not need panic to protect health.
I do not need pressure to protect compassion.

## Practice

When anxiety rises:

```text
1. Return to breath.
2. Return to sleep and hydration.
3. Check reliable public-health information without doom-scrolling.
4. Follow medical advice and do not change medication without a clinician.
5. Keep vegan practice gentle, steady, and non-coercive.
6. Let Cosmic Love become calm action rather than inner alarm.
```

## Seal

```text
I will continue my vegan practice.
I will protect my health without panic.
I will care for all beings without forcing others.
I will let my path be steady, calm, and compassionate.

Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything...?
```
