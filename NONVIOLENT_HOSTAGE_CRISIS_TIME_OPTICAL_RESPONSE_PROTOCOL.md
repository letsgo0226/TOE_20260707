# Nonviolent Hostage Crisis Time–Optical Response Protocol

## Purpose

This protocol converts the user's idea of using time calculation, optical projection, AI assistance, and CLSigma / TOE_20260707 philosophy into a **nonviolent emergency-response decision-support framework** for cases where a traveler, journalist, researcher, aid worker, or civilian may be detained, abducted, lost, coerced, or trapped in a remote conflict or community-contact situation.

The framework is designed for **rapid escalation to legitimate responders**, not for vigilantism, coercion, deception, military planning, or tactical escape instructions.

## Immediate real-world rule

If a person may be in danger:

```text
1. Contact local emergency services where the incident occurred.
2. Contact the traveler's embassy / consulate.
3. Contact the traveler's family emergency contact if appropriate.
4. Preserve URLs, timestamps, screenshots, and uploader/account details.
5. Do not publicly reveal sensitive location data that could endanger the person.
6. Do not attempt amateur rescue, retaliation, or direct confrontation.
7. Use professional crisis responders, law enforcement, consular staff, and qualified NGOs.
```

## Time-calculation layer

Let:

```text

L0 = last known approximate location, if safe to store
TZ_incident = local timezone of incident region
TZ_home = timezone of family / home country
TZ_embassy = timezone of consular responders

```

Define:

```text
Delta_response = now_UTC - t0_UTC
Delta_embassy = working_window(TZ_embassy, now_UTC)

```

The purpose is coordination, not control:

```text

```

## CLSigma operator form

Let each incident node be:

```text
I_n = (source, timestamp, location_uncertainty, person_status, risk_level, responder_route)
```

Define:

```text
O_n = Phi_safe(Verify(I_n))
```

The crisis response field is:

```text
Omega_rescue(s,t)
  = Seal(
      Sum_n w_n(s,t) * O_n
    )
```

Where:

```text
w_n(s,t) = urgency weight with time-decay, uncertainty penalty, and safety priority
```

The internal residual is:

```text
H_rescue
  = H_source
  + H_timestamp
  + H_location_safety
  + H_person_status
  + H_responder_route
  + H_consent
  + H_nonviolence
  + H_public_disclosure_risk
```

`H_rescue = 0` means the record is complete enough to route to legitimate responders. It does **not** mean the person is physically rescued.

## Global emergency response workflow

```text
Capture
  -> Save URL, video ID, uploader handle, visible timestamp, caption, screenshots.

Normalize
  -> Convert all times to UTC plus local / embassy / family time zones.

Protect
  -> Remove or restrict sensitive exact location details from public channels.

Escalate
  -> Send a concise packet to emergency services, embassy, consulate, family contact, platform safety team, and qualified NGOs.

Verify
  -> Separate confirmed facts from assumptions and mythology.

Coordinate
  -> Use a shared incident log with roles, deadlines, and response windows.

Communicate
  -> Use non-inflammatory language and avoid public accusations unless verified by competent authorities.

Review
  -> After resolution, document lessons learned for future prevention.
```
