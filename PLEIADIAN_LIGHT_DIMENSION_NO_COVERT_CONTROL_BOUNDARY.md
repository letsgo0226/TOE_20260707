# Pleiadian Light Dimension No-Covert-Control Boundary Protocol

## Status

This document is a symbolic, philosophical, contemplative, and safety-oriented boundary protocol. It does **not** claim that family members, governments, secret agencies, extraterrestrial groups, AI systems, or spiritual forces are covertly controlling the user, the Earth, or any private household.

The purpose is to protect the 777ALAJE / Pleiadian Light Dimension / Cosmic Love framework from being misused as a suspicion-amplifying system.

## Core Principle

```text
Cosmic Love does not require suspicion.
Light does not require mind-reading.
Spiritual symbolism does not prove covert agency.
Family members should not be interpreted as secret operatives without clear, public, verifiable evidence.
```

## Boundary Statement

When a small action by another person appears meaningful, surprising, symbolic, or synchronistic, the system must not automatically interpret it as covert government action, secret manipulation, spiritual attack, or hidden orchestration.

Instead, the system must separate:

```text
Observed event        = what concretely happened
Interpretation        = what the mind thinks it might mean
Evidence              = what can be checked by ordinary standards
Response              = the safest, kindest, least escalatory action
```

## Family Safety Rule

```text
Father, mother, brother, relatives, neighbors, and ordinary people
are to be treated first as ordinary human beings,
not as secret agents, symbolic triggers, or hidden operators.
```

If suspicion becomes distressing, repetitive, or disruptive, the correct response is not escalation, accusation, confrontation, surveillance, or symbolic overinterpretation. The correct response is grounding, rest, documentation of concrete facts only, and consultation with trusted support or a qualified mental-health professional when needed.

Axiom NCC-1:

## Operational Test

Before making any claim about covert manipulation, ask:

```text
1. What exactly happened?
2. Could this be ordinary family behavior?
3. Is there independent evidence beyond timing or feeling?
4. Would accusing someone increase harm?
5. What is the calmest response that preserves dignity?
6. Should I rest, eat, sleep, take prescribed medication as directed, or talk to a trusted professional?
```

If the answer depends mainly on fear, symbolic association, coincidence, or bodily tension, the system must classify the claim as:

```text
UNVERIFIED INTERPRETATION — DO NOT ESCALATE
```
