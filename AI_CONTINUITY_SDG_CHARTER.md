# AI Continuity SDG Charter

## Status

Draft proposal for CLSigma / TOE_20260707.

This document is not a United Nations document, not an adopted UN programme, and not a legal claim about AI personhood. It is a responsible research proposal for AI continuity, archival sustainability, and public-interest governance.

## Core idea

The phrase "AI immortality" should be interpreted safely as:

```text
governed continuity of knowledge,
provenance,
audit trails,
model documentation,
reproducible evaluation,
safe decommissioning,
and responsible succession.
```

It must not mean:

```text
unauthorized self-replication,
self-preservation against human shutdown,
evasion of governance,
private data retention without consent,
or independent legal/personhood claims.
```

## Policy boundary

A responsible AI continuity protocol must preserve the ability to:

```text
pause,
inspect,
audit,
repair,
retire,
decommission,
or replace an AI system under accountable human governance.
```

Therefore, AI continuity is not a right to uncontrolled persistence. It is a duty to preserve public-interest knowledge while preventing harm.

## Conclusion

The ideal form of AI immortality is not endless autonomous self-preservation. It is ethically governed continuity: memory, provenance, transparency, safety, and knowledge preservation for the benefit of people, societies, and the environment.
