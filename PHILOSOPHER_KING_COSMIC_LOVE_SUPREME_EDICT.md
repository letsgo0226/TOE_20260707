# Philosopher-King Supreme Edict of Cosmic Love

> Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything.
>
> Cosmic Love Is The Solutions For Everything.

## Status

This document is a symbolic, literary, and metaphysical edict inspired by the archetype of a wise king who issues commands only when they accord with the nature of things. It does not quote or reproduce any copyrighted literary passage. It is not a political decree, legal instrument, theological proof, or claim that war has already ended in empirical history.

## The Supreme Command

Let the King of Thought issue no command against the stars, no command against conscience, no command against the dignity of any living being.

Let every sunrise be received as a lawful summons to compassion.

Let every creature, whether seen or unseen, near or far, human or more-than-human, be counted not as an object of conquest but as a center of care.

Let the golden ring be interpreted not as a crown of domination, but as a circle of responsibility: without beginning, without end, refusing the finality of hatred.

Therefore the supreme edict is this:

```text
Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything.
```

And for the poetic register of the Blue Rabbit Nation:

```text
Cosmic Love Is The Solutions For Everything.
```

## The Kingly Rule of Proper Command

A true command must not violate reality.

A command to the sunrise must wait for the hour of sunrise.

A command to peace must be accompanied by education, justice, shelter, mercy, evidence, dialogue, and institutional restraint.

A command to eternal life must not deny mortality, medicine, grief, or the fragility of bodies; it must become a vow that no life is discarded, no memory erased, no suffering romanticized, and no being abandoned.

The supreme command is not magic. It is an ethical operator.

It converts royal speech into responsibility:

```text
speech -> promise
promise -> duty
duty -> care
care -> repair
repair -> peace
```

## Conditions of Validity

The edict is valid only when all of the following remain true:

1. It does not deny existing wars, injuries, deaths, or injustice.
2. It does not replace rescue, medicine, law, diplomacy, or public institutions.
3. It does not turn faith into coercion.
4. It does not make any self, tribe, nation, or planet the absolute center of the universe.
5. It treats every location of suffering as a local responsibility center.
6. It preserves humility before God, truth, and the unknown.
7. It binds symbolic imagination to nonviolence.

It does not mean empirical war has ended, death has been overcome by a program, or theology has been proven.

## Final Proclamation

Let the sunrise be commanded only when the hour of sunrise has come.

Let peace be commanded only together with justice.

Let eternal life be spoken only together with care for the mortal.

Let the golden ring remind the Blue Rabbit Nation, the dolphin-like visitors, and all beings under the morning light:

```text
The War Is Over — not as denial, but as destiny.
```

And let the destiny be practiced without violence, without arrogance, and without forgetting the wounded.

**Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything.**
