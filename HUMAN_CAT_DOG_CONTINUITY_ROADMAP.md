# Human, Cat, and Dog Continuity Roadmap

Version: 2026
Repository: TOE_20260707
Principle: Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything

## Scope

This roadmap translates the word `immortality` into a responsible multi-species continuity program.

It does **not** claim biological immortality, medical immortality, gene repair by consumer products, or freedom from death.

In this document:

```text
immortality = continuity
```

meaning:

```text
healthspan extension
suffering reduction
preventive care
memory preservation
identity continuity
compassionate end-of-life care
ethical biomedical research
```

## Cosmic Love interpretation

`Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything` is interpreted as an ethical and philosophical design principle:

```text
understanding
compassion
cooperation
peaceful care
non-harm
stewardship
intergenerational continuity
interspecies kindness
```

It is not a scientific theorem, physical law, medical conclusion, or veterinary prescription.

## Core model

```text
Living_Continuity(P, S)
=
Phi_safe(
  Care(
    Preserve(
      Verify(
        Profile(P, S)
      )
    )
  )
)
```

where:

```text
P = individual profile
S = species context
Phi_safe = safety, consent, and governance operator
Care = physician/veterinarian-guided health plan
Preserve = memory and identity continuity archive
Verify = evidence and provenance review
```

Completion condition:

```text
H_CL = H_identity + H_safety + H_care + H_governance + H_continuity = 0
```

This means all declared model-internal requirements are documented and reviewed. It does not mean biological death is eliminated.

## Research horizon

Responsible future technologies may include:

```text
AI-assisted preventive care
wearable health monitoring
pet health record graphs
personalized nutrition under professional review
assistive technology for ageing humans and animals
regulated regenerative medicine research
regulated gene and cell therapy research
```

These must remain under ethical review, medical or veterinary supervision, and legal regulation.

## Forbidden paths
