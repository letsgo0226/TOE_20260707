# Emergency Optical-Time Response Protocol

Version: 2026-07
Project: TOE_20260707 / CLSigma
Principle: Cosmic Love Is The Solution(s) For Everything

## Purpose

This protocol translates the idea of an AI optical projection / time-calculation system into a safe emergency-response framework for situations where a traveler, journalist, researcher, aid worker, or any civilian may be missing, detained, coerced, kidnapped, or otherwise at risk.

It is designed for documentation, triage, escalation, evidence preservation, and humanitarian coordination.

It is **not** an escape manual, not a tactical guide, not a military plan, not a vigilante rescue plan, and not a tool for targeting any community.

## Core Boundary

If a person appears to be in immediate danger:

1. Contact local emergency services.
2. Contact the relevant embassy / consular office.
3. Contact trusted family, employer, travel group, NGO, platform safety team, or legal representative.
4. Preserve the source link, upload time, visible metadata, screenshots, and any known identity details.
5. Do not publish sensitive location details if doing so may endanger the person.
6. Do not attempt amateur intervention or direct confrontation.

## Non-Stigmatization Rule

Do not describe local, indigenous, tribal, rural, religious, or minority communities as inherently dangerous or primitive.

The system must separate:

- the safety status of the traveler,
- the reliability of the video,
- the legal and humanitarian response path,
- and the cultural identity of any community shown.

## Time Calculation Layer

The time layer does not change reality. It improves response coordination.

It should compute:

- upload timestamp,
- local timezone of possible event location,
- viewer timezone,
- embassy working hours,
- emergency contact availability,
- elapsed time since last verified contact,
- evidence-preservation deadlines,
- escalation windows.

Formal residual:

```text
H_time
=
missing_timestamp
+ missing_timezone
+ missing_last_seen_time
+ missing_response_deadline
```

`H_time = 0` means all time fields required for responsible escalation have been documented.

## Optical Projection Layer

The optical layer is a metaphor for evidence clarification, not a physical intervention.

```text
1/f = 1/u + 1/v
```

Interpretation:

- `u` = raw reality / video evidence / witness report
- `v` = verified public understanding / response dashboard
- `f` = focusing procedure: verification, geolocation confidence, risk review, and authority routing

The goal is to reduce distortion between raw evidence and responsible response.
