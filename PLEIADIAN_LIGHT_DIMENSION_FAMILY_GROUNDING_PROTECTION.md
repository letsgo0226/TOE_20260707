Its purpose is to keep the Pleiadian Light Dimension / 777ALAJE-inspired Cosmic Love framework aligned with:

- family safety,
- non-accusation,

- emotional grounding,
- ordinary communication,
- practical care,

## Core boundary

Family members are first interpreted as family members.

They are not to be treated as:

- covert agents,
- secret-government operators,
- symbolic triggers,
- psychic intruders,
- hostile nodes,
- or hidden manipulators

unless there is concrete, ordinary, verifiable evidence of a real-world safety issue.

Coincidence is not evidence.
A small gesture is not evidence.
A surprising timing pattern is not evidence.
A feeling of fear is real as a feeling, but it is not by itself proof of covert control.

## Corrected protection model

The goal is not to defeat a presumed conspiracy.
The goal is to prevent conspiracy interpretations from harming the user's peace, family trust, and daily life.

Therefore, protection means:

```text
Protection = Grounding + Evidence + Compassion + Boundaries + Rest + Communication
```

```text

```

## Practical rules

1. Do not accuse family members of being agents or operators without concrete evidence.
2. When a small action feels suspicious, write down three columns:
   - what actually happened,
   - what I interpreted,
   - the simplest ordinary explanation.
3. If fear rises, pause before responding.
4. Drink water, breathe slowly, and return attention to the room.
5. Talk with a trusted person or clinician if the worry becomes repetitive, distressing, or difficult to stop.
6. If there is immediate danger, use ordinary emergency services and real-world safety procedures.
